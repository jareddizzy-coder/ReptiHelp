1. Introduction

ReptiHelp ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). By using ReptiHelp, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

  • Email Address — used for creating and managing user accounts.
  • Password — stored in encrypted form for secure authentication.
  • ZIP Code — used to find nearby veterinarians using map services.

We may also collect anonymous usage data such as device type, operating system, and performance data for analytics and app improvement.

3. How We Use Information

  • To create and manage accounts.
  • To display local veterinary services based on ZIP code.

  • To communicate updates, bug fixes, and support responses.

4. Data Storage and Security

All personal data is securely stored using Google Cloud services. We use encryption and other industry-standard measures to protect your data. While we strive for maximum security, no online system is 100% secure.

5. Children’s Privacy

ReptiHelp may be used by users under 13 years old with parental supervision. We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). If a child under 13 submits personal data without consent, please contact us to have it deleted.

6. Third-Party Services

ReptiHelp uses services provided by Google, including Google Cloud, Google Maps, and Google Play Services. These providers may collect and process data as described in their own privacy policies. Please review Google’s policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data. Information is shared only when necessary to provide core app functions or when legally required.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your state, you may have the right to access, delete, or restrict use of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at jareddizzy@gmail.com.
